Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 25, 2026

Information we process

Time24 is designed to work without mandatory account creation. We may process limited technical information such as browser type, approximate location from timezone settings, and page interaction events for performance and product improvement.

Cookies and analytics

We may use analytics tools (such as Google Analytics) to understand aggregate traffic and usage behavior. These tools may use cookies or similar identifiers.

Advertising

We may display ads through Google AdSense or related Google services. Google and its partners may use cookies to serve personalized or non-personalized ads based on your visit and other sites. You can review ad controls through your Google Ad settings.

Local storage

Some features (theme choice, alarm/task settings, preferences) may be stored locally in your browser to improve your user experience.

Third-party services

Time24 uses public APIs (sunrise-sunset.org, ipapi.co) and CDNs (unpkg, jsDelivr, Google Fonts) to deliver core functionality. These providers may log your IP address and request metadata for abuse prevention.

Contact

For privacy requests or concerns, email support@time24.co.in.
